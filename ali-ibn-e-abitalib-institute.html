Our Story

About the Institute

A place of learning, reflection, and community — rooted in faith and guided by scholarship.

Ali ibn-e-Abitalib Islamic Institute, established in 2018 in Quetta, Pakistan, is dedicated to promoting Islamic knowledge and community engagement. The institute hosts Islamic lectures, Quran lessons, youth programs, and special events according to the Islamic calendar. Under the guidance of Allama Muhammad Kazim Behjati, the institute serves as a place where people gather to learn and strengthen their understanding of Islam.

Leadership

Our Founder

Guided by scholarly wisdom and a lifelong dedication to the community.

Allama Muhammad Kazim Behjati

Founder & Head of the Institute

Under the visionary leadership of Allama Muhammad Kazim Behjati, the Ali ibn-e-Abitalib Islamic Institute was founded in 1992 with a mission to provide authentic Islamic education and foster a strong sense of community. His dedication to scholarship, spiritual guidance, and community service has been the driving force behind the institute's growth and impact in Quetta, Balochistan.
